Welcome To the Social Painter Web Page, Guide To the page:
you can draw 1 pixel in a minute, to draw a pixel double right click with the on the desired pixel,
If you want to know the time until you can draw the next pixel, look at the bottom right corner (top to the tooltips)
To change the color to draw the pixel you can:
  • Press the matched color button in the toolbox (bottom of the screen)
  • using buttons to move the left or right color in the toolbox, and if you come to the end it goes to the other side
    • Z to move to the relative left color
    • C to move to the relative right color
If you want to move on the board either:
  • Hold the and drag the board
  • Use the Key Arrows
To zoom on the board either:
  • zooming with the wheel
  • zoom in with Shift Key
  • zoom out with Shift Key
  • There are default zoom options, the glass button in the left bottom corner, can
Remember you can't scale the board less then 0.5 or more then 50 times.
